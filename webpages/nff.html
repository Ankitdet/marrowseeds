   NFF - Nature First by Farmers  

Why NFF?

In the current scenario, we have developed cultivars that give the best output using only and only man-made artificial/harmful resources. So it gives the best result only when you apply artificial/harmful resources. So firstly, it's very important to develop natural resource-responsive cultivars that give the best output using only natural resources.

NFF Cultivars

It's the cultivars that can give the best output with utilization of natural resources like sun, soil, water, and bio resources only.

Why NFF Cultivars?

We can maintain or increase the soil health and human health directly or indirectly. It's the first step towards caring for nature against man-made disturbance.

Benefits of NFF Cultivars
  • Reduction in cost of cultivation
  • Increase profit margin for farmers
  • Maintain or improve the soil health
  • Creation of a sustainable ecosystem using natural resources
  • Improve human health through minimizing the use of man-made harmful resources
Hurdles in the Creation of the NFF Ecosystem
  • It takes time, hard work, and wealth to develop NFF cultivars
  • It's a tedious and lengthy process involving morals, interests, and positivity of the people engaged in its development
Possible Ways to Overcome the Hurdles
  • Region/cluster of people-based research farms
  • Product/crop-based research farms
  • Guidance from a mentor/nonprofit organization with funding, interest, and skillful manpower

At least we have to start it. Let's do it together.
